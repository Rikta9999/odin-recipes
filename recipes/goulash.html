Goulash

Description

Goulashis a soup or stew of meat and vegetables seasoned with paprika and other spices.

Originating in Hungary, goulash is a common meal predominantly eaten in Central Europe but also in other parts of Europe. It is one of the national dishes of Hungary and a symbol of the country.

Ingredients

  • 500g of beef meat
  • 500g of potatoes
  • 2 onions
  • 3 carrots
  • Spices: salt, pepper, paprika
  • 500ml of water
  • Oil

Steps

  1. Pour the oil in the hot pot
  2. Fry the diced onions and carrots in the oil on low heat
  3. After 10 minutes add the beef and continue cooking
  4. After 5 minutes add the water and spices
  5. Cook for 2 hours on low heat
